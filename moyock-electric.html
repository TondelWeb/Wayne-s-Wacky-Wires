Wayne's Wacky Wires – Electrical Services

Full-service residential and commercial electrical work — done right, on time, and at a fair price.

Service 01

Electrical Repairs

From a dead outlet to a breaker that keeps tripping, electrical problems are frustrating — and sometimes dangerous. Our electricians diagnose issues quickly and fix them right the first time, so you can get back to normal without the headache.

Benefits

  • Fast diagnosis and same-day repairs in most cases
  • Eliminates fire and shock hazards from faulty wiring
  • Upfront pricing with no surprise charges
  • All work meets current NC electrical code
When you need this:

Outlets that don't work, breakers that trip repeatedly, flickering lights, warm or sparking switches, or anything that just doesn't seem right.

🔧
Service 02

Panel Upgrades

Older homes often have 100-amp panels that simply can't handle modern electrical loads. We upgrade your panel to 200 amps (or more) safely and professionally, including all necessary permits and inspections.

Benefits

  • Supports modern appliances, EV chargers, and home additions
  • Replaces outdated or recalled panel brands (Federal Pacific, Zinsco)
  • Increases home safety and resale value
  • Permits pulled and inspection coordinated by us
When you need this:

Installing an EV charger, adding a hot tub or home addition, buying or selling an older home, or experiencing frequent tripped breakers.

⚡
Service 03

Wiring & Rewiring

Whether you're building new construction, adding a room, or replacing dangerous old wiring, we handle it all. Knob-and-tube and aluminum branch circuit wiring both need to be replaced — we do that safely with minimal disruption.

Benefits

  • New construction rough-in and trim-out
  • Full or partial rewiring of existing homes
  • Eliminates fire risk from aluminum wiring or degraded insulation
  • Adds outlets and circuits where you need them
When you need this:

Home renovations, older homes with two-prong outlets throughout, home additions, or an insurance company flagging old wiring.

🔌
Service 04

Lighting Installation

Good lighting transforms a space. We install recessed lighting, ceiling fans, under-cabinet lights, outdoor security lighting, and EV charging stations. Clean work, no unnecessary drywall cuts, done right the first time.

Benefits

  • Indoor recessed, pendant, and ceiling fan installation
  • Outdoor floodlights, landscape lighting, and motion sensors
  • LED retrofit and dimmer upgrades
  • EV charger installation (Level 2, 240V)
When you need this:

Remodeling a kitchen or bathroom, improving curb appeal and security with exterior lighting, or switching to an electric vehicle.

💡
Service 05

Troubleshooting & Inspections

Electrical issues can be tricky to trace. We have the tools and experience to find the source of any problem. We also perform pre-purchase electrical inspections with written reports that buyers, sellers, and real estate agents rely on.

Benefits

  • Written inspection reports accepted by home buyers and insurers
  • Identifies hidden hazards before they become emergencies
  • Helps buyers negotiate or make informed decisions
  • Thorough, unbiased assessment from a licensed electrician
When you need this:

Buying or selling a home, getting new homeowner's insurance, or you suspect an electrical issue but can't pinpoint it.

🔍
Service 06

Emergency Electrical Service

Some electrical problems can't wait until Monday morning. We offer emergency response throughout Currituck County and surrounding areas — because when there's a burning smell, sparking outlet, or total power loss, you need a real electrician fast.

Benefits

  • Fast response times — typically under an hour locally
  • Available evenings and weekends for true emergencies
  • Prioritizes your safety first, repairs second
  • No price gouging — fair emergency rates, always disclosed upfront
When you need this:

Burning smell from outlets or panel, sparking wires, complete loss of power to your home, or any situation where you feel unsafe.

Our Story

Built on Hard Work & Honest Service

My name is Mike Carver, and I've been an electrician for over 15 years. I grew up right here in Currituck County — went to school at Currituck County High, and after working for a larger electrical company in Chesapeake for nearly a decade, I knew I wanted to come back home and do things my own way.

I started Wayne's Wacky Wires in 2009 with one truck, a few good tools, and a simple philosophy: show up on time, do the job right, charge a fair price, and never leave a customer with a question unanswered. Fifteen years later, that's still exactly how we operate.

We're not a huge company and we don't pretend to be. What we are is reliable. When you call us, you're talking to someone who knows Moyock, knows the houses out here, and genuinely cares about doing right by his neighbors. Most of our business comes from referrals — and that says everything.

Today I work with a small crew of trusted electricians, all local guys who take the same pride in their work that I do. We're fully licensed by the state of North Carolina and carry liability insurance and workers' comp on every job.
